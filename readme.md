## 🌟 Featured Projects

### 🤝 AI Social App — Helping People Feel Less Lonely
A human-centered AI social app designed to support people who feel lonely by encouraging meaningful self-expression, reflection, and connection.

- 🎙 Voice-based conversations with an AI agent
- 🧠 After each conversation, the system generates insight cards that capture:
  - emotionally grounded reflections
  - concrete memory anchors (scenes, quotes, actions)
  - recurring personal patterns & themes
- 🔁 These cards form a structured record of the user's growth and can be used to support future matching & deeper connections

**Tech stack:** Flutter • Next.js • Supabase • LLM pipeline • embeddings & retrieval
